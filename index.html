About Me

The Story Behind the Strategy

My SEO Journey

My path into SEO started with curiosity about a simple question: why do some websites rank effortlessly while others, with better products and services, stay invisible on page two? That question turned into a career. Over the past three-plus years, I've worked hands-on with businesses across home services, healthcare, fitness, and renewable energy — auditing, rebuilding, and optimizing websites so they earn the visibility their businesses deserve.

Why I Chose SEO

SEO sits at the intersection of data, psychology, and technology — exactly where I do my best work. I chose SEO because it rewards curiosity and precision in equal measure: you need to understand how search engines crawl and interpret a website, how users actually search and behave, and how business goals translate into measurable growth. Few disciplines let you be equal parts analyst, strategist, and problem-solver.

My Passion for Technical Optimization

Technical SEO is where I feel most at home. There's a genuine thrill in tracing a crawl budget problem back to a redirect chain, or watching a Core Web Vitals score climb after a round of image and script optimization. I care about the details other specialists skip — canonical tags, schema markup, log file patterns — because those details compound into real ranking and revenue gains.

My Work Philosophy

I believe SEO should never be a black box. Every recommendation I make is backed by data, tied to a business outcome, and explained in plain language. I combine technical expertise with a user-first mindset — because the sites that rank best are ultimately the sites that serve people best.
